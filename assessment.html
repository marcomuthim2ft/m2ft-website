Before you upload

1. Replace the email address

Find this line in the form:

action="https://formsubmit.co/YOUR_EMAIL@example.com"

Replace it with your real email address.

2. Upload this file

Upload this file to your GitHub repo as a page like assessment.html or rename it to index.html.

3. Activate email delivery

The first submission will trigger an activation email. Confirm it once, then future submissions go to your inbox.

This version is intentionally simple and reliable. It captures the lead first and lets you generate the programme manually.
