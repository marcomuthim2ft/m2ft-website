Meet Your Coach

MSc-qualified performance coach with elite-level experience

FOUNDER & HEAD COACH

Marco Muthi

Marco is an FA-qualified coach and performance specialist with a background in Sport & Exercise Science (University of Bath) and an MSc in Performance Football Coaching from St Mary's University.

Having worked across academy, grassroots, and semi-professional environments — including roles with Chelsea FC Foundation, Aldershot Town, and LionHeart Football — Marco combines science-backed training with real-world experience. His mission through M2FT is to help players and athletes unlock elite performance through structured, purposeful coaching both on and off the pitch.
