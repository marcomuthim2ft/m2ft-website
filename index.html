Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need To Know

If you’re unsure which option is right for you, start here. These are the most common questions players and parents ask before choosing a programme.

The free 4-week programme gives you a personalised written training plan built around your assessment. The paid options upgrade the experience with a more premium report format, improved presentation, video support, and optional coach input depending on the package you choose.
The M2FT Pro Programme gives you a more premium version of your plan, including a stronger visual layout, clearer player summary, and a more professional report-style structure that is easier to follow and more valuable to keep.
The Programme + Video option includes selected drill clips matched to your position, goals, and main training focus. This helps you understand how the work should look, how to apply it properly, and what key details to focus on during each session.
This is the highest-support option. It includes everything in the Programme + Video package, plus added coach input, priority turnaround, and one follow-up adjustment if needed. It is designed for players who want a more coach-led experience and extra reassurance.
Programmes are typically delivered within 1–2 days after your assessment is submitted. If you choose a higher package with more support, your delivery still aims to stay fast while allowing time for proper review and programme build.
