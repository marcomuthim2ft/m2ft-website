Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session?

Fill out the contact form above or email us directly at Marco@officialm2ft.com. We'll get back to you within 24 hours to discuss availability and schedule your first session.

What should I bring to my first session?

For football: boots, trainers, shin pads, water bottle, and weather-appropriate kit. For fitness: trainers, water bottle, and comfortable workout clothes.

Do you offer trial sessions?

Yes! Your first fitness class is FREE. For 1-to-1 coaching, get in touch to discuss a trial session.

Where are sessions held?

We operate at Nescot Sports Centre (Epsom), Auriol Park (Stoneleigh), and other locations across Surrey. Specific venues are confirmed when you book.

What's your cancellation policy?

You must inform us at least 24 hours before your session. We can reschedule or refund you. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice incur a 50% charge.

Do you offer online coaching?

Yes! Download the M2FT app for online programmes, video demonstrations, and remote coaching support.
