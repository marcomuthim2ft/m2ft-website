Position-Specific Training

We use position-specific skill requirements (Hughes et al., 2012) to structure every session

Centre Backs

Defending, aerial duels, positioning, distribution

Full-Backs

1v1 defending, overlapping runs, crossing, stamina

Holding Midfielders

Positioning, interceptions, passing range, composure

Attacking Midfielders

Creativity, through balls, dribbling, shooting

Wingers

1v1 skills, crossing, pace, cutting inside

Strikers

Finishing, movement, hold-up play, positioning
